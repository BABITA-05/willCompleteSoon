Video Compilation

Video compilation is the creative process of gathering multiple video clips and combining them into a single, cohesive presentation. It often involves selecting highlights, arranging them in a logical or artistic sequence, and adding transitions, music, or text to enhance the storytelling. Whether used for entertainment, education, or documentation, video compilations allow creators to transform raw footage into engaging content that captures attention and conveys a clear message.
